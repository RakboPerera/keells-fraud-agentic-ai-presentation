# Fraud Intelligence Ecosystem

## Fraud Risk Intelligence Framework

The Agentic AI Fraud Risk Intelligence framework organizes fraud monitoring into a set of specialized analytical agents that continuously observe operational activity across the retail value chain.

Rather than relying solely on static rules or operational dashboards, these agents analyze behavioral patterns across large datasets to identify anomalies that may indicate operational misuse or fraud.

Each agent focuses on a specific operational environment within the grocery retail ecosystem.

Together these agents form a coordinated Fraud Risk Intelligence Platform capable of detecting emerging operational risks and supporting investigation teams.

---

## Core Fraud Intelligence Domains

The fraud intelligence framework includes five primary monitoring domains.

### Transaction Integrity Intelligence

The Transaction Integrity Agent monitors point-of-sale transaction activity across stores to detect behavioral anomalies related to cashier actions, refunds, price overrides and transaction voids.

This domain focuses on identifying unusual transaction behavior patterns that may indicate manipulation or misuse.

---

### Promotion and Discount Integrity Intelligence

Promotions and discounts are critical components of retail strategy but also introduce opportunities for misuse.

The Promotion Integrity Agent monitors promotion eligibility rules, discount application patterns and cashier-level behavior to detect potential violations of promotion policies.

---

### Inventory and Shrinkage Intelligence

Inventory shrinkage represents one of the largest sources of financial leakage in grocery retail operations.

The Inventory Integrity Agent analyzes product movement across the supply chain including deliveries, stock adjustments, wastage reporting and recorded sales.

By reconciling these operational flows the system can detect emerging discrepancies that may indicate shrinkage risk.

---

### Procurement and Supplier Risk Intelligence

Procurement activities may expose retailers to financial risk through supplier pricing anomalies, preferential vendor selection or invoice discrepancies.

The Supplier Risk Agent analyzes procurement activity across suppliers, categories and purchase orders to identify unusual pricing patterns or supplier relationships.

---

### Nexus Loyalty Integrity Intelligence

Loyalty programs generate additional operational signals that can indicate unusual behavioral patterns.

The Nexus Integrity Agent monitors the lifecycle of loyalty points across transactions including purchases, points accumulation and refunds.

This enables detection of scenarios where points accumulation does not align with underlying purchase activity.

---

## The Fraud Intelligence Platform

These five agents operate together as part of a unified Fraud Intelligence Platform.

The platform continuously:

- monitors operational events across systems
- analyzes behavioral patterns across time
- detects deviations from expected behavior
- generates contextual investigation summaries

This approach transforms fraud detection from reactive investigation into continuous operational intelligence embedded within retail systems.
